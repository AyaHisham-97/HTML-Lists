5 Reasons Why I Love Music

  1. It helps me to overcome my depression.

    2. I have a depression. Depression is a severe illness for me because it may cause death. Some may say that depression is just a joke or a drama of the person who's having it. Treating it is costly so I have to find an alternative solution to overcome it. Then there's music. Everytime my depression visits me, I have just to listen to music or to my favorite songs to be specific. After listening I just have to do three deep breaths and then eventually my depression would be gone.


  2. Music is my great comforter.

    3. There is one time that I am so down that I cried all night. Then there's music, it never left me. I just listened to it and then started thinking positively. I didn't realized I am already smiling. When no one is there for me to comfort me, I will just get my earphones and then listen to music. Second by second, minute by minute, I forgot my worries and negative thoughts. Music is just a great comforter to me.
  3. It helps me to have more focus on something.

    4. Sometimes when I have many things to do, I can't focus on one thing. My mind is struggling. If I am not focused I can't do my best on that thing. So there's one time that I have many things to do. I am out of focus because I am thinking about what to do first. So I listened to music. I didn't know that I was so focused that I finished all my works on time beautifully. So everytime I have things to do. I just turn up the music and start doing it.
  4. When I can't express my feelings, there's music to help me express it.

    5. There's a time in our lives that we just can't express our feelings to ourselves and to everyone. Whether we are sad, happy, or angry. I have that time too. When I got broken, I can't speak. I cried and cried. Until I see my guitar in the corner of my room. I get it and start strumming it. I sang my feelings out. It helped me to be calm and to move on. Until then, I used music as a language when I can't express my feelings.
  5. It serves as a foundation of my family.

    6. Since my family is musically inclined, our bonding time is through music. Without music, we may not able to communicate to each other personally. I am glad and very thankful that God has given me a precious gift, my family. I may not have a perfect family but if we sing together we will produce a perfect harmony.
